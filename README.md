## Redefining Luxury in Kurunegala

The **Bravon Sky Hotel** represents an architectural masterpiece rising gracefully beneath the iconic silhouette of Elephant Rock (Athugala). Fusing modern climate comfort with traditional Ceylon tropical layouts, Bravon Sky stands as a premium retreat for discerning business travelers and cultural explorers alike.

### The Experience

*   **Premium Suites:** 17 thoughtfully crafted units featuring private open-air balconies, custom Sri Lankan wooden craftsmanship, and spectacular valley views.
*   **Aurora Sky Lounge:** Kurunegala's premier rooftop dining escape, offering panoramic views, open-air terraces, and Sri Lankan fusion gastronomy under spectacular dusks.
*   **Tranquil Ecology:** Engineered with a net-zero carbon design philosophy, featuring solar arrays, natural green ventilation corridors, and lush indoor plant life.
*   **Strategic Access:** Perfectly positioned to explore Kurunegala Lake, Athugala Hill, and ancient cultural landmarks like the Yapahuwa Citadel.
